Meal for children:-

  • Base your meals on starchy carbohydrates, such as wholemeal pasta, brown rice or potatoes.
  • Have at least 5 portions of fruits and vegetables a day.
  • Have some lean protein from lean meat, fish, beans and pulses.
  • Have dairy products such as whole milk, yoghurt, cheese until you build your back up.
  • Eats larger than normal portions.
  • Select higher calorie foods and the calories should be uptill 2600.
  • Good night sleep of upto 8 to 10 hrs.
  • Make sure you drink plenty of water. Milkshakes(Chocolate, banana, apple etc),dairy products. .
