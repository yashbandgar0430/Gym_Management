For Weight Gain:-

  • Don’t wait until your stomach starts to growl to eat.
  • Pack calorie-rich snacks you can eat on the go, like bananas and peanut butter or dense granola bars.
  • Eat food that’s nutrient-rich and nourishing.
  • Avoid getting frozen dinners, fast food and snack foods, which contain a lot of salt, sugar and other fillers that aren’t nutritious.
  • Cook as many meals from scratch as you can.
  • Focus on protein, fats, and carbohydrates.
  • Make sure you drink plenty of water. drink unsweetened tea, 4 to 8 ounces of fruit juice, flavored water, and other healthy beverages. Avoid drinking Gatorade and other sports drinks in excessive amounts, since they contain a lot of sugar.
