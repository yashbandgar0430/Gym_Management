WEIGHT GAIN EXERCISE


1.Dead bug

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x15 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-it improves balance and stability. It can improve the control on the spine and the posture while the body is in motion or if it is still.



2.Superman

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-can help lifters establish isometric muscle contractions in the glutes and lower back (spinal erectors) which can be
helpful for loaded movements like the squat and deadlift, as well as more dynamic movements such as running, swimming, and most spotting movements.



3.Plank

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-Plank and plank variation exercises activate all the core muscles, including the rectus abdominis,
transverse abdominis, and obliques. They also activate the muscles in the hips and back.



4.Leg lowers

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-Raising your legs helps blood to circulate more efficiently and relieves pressure off your lower extremities, giving your heart a much-needed break from having to do all the work.



5.Glute bridge

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-Glute Activation. Glute activation is a huge goal of many squat and deadlift warm up series.



6.Single leg deadfit left and right

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-Muscular Strength and Endurance. This unilateral deadlift variation is a great way to increase muscular hypertrophy and endurance in the hamstrings and glutes.



7.Step up on to chair

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x15 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-Step-up onto the chair is an exercise for balance and improving the strength of your lower body, quadriceps, glutes.



8.Jump squat

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-This is the best exercise for the gluts, quads, calves and abs.



9.Lunges

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-Balance and stability. Lunges are a lower body unilateral exercise since you work on each side of your body independently.
Balance and stability. Lunges are a lower body unilateral exercise since you work on each side of your body independently.



10.Jumping jacks

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-improve your coordination.Burn Fat.



11.Jump Rope

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x20 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:- Engages your core, fires up your quads and glutes while involving little shoulder and arm action that improves your
cardiovascular health and body coordination.



12.Pushups

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:-x15 or 30 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-This is the best exercise to tone your shoulders, chest, triceps, and abs



13.Bench dips

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x30 or 45 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-Bench dips can help you build muscle in your upper body.



14.Rotating T Extensions

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- x30 or 45 sec (repeat 2 or 3 sets if possible)
Benificial for:-This is a great exercise to perform to improve spinal health. It increases T-spine mobility with minimal involvement of the low back



15.Running

Workout Days:- atleast 3-4 days in week
Time Duration:- 20-30 min
Benificial for:-Keep You Healthy. Running is one of the best ways to boost your overall health.
